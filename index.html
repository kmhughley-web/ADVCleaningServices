ADVanced Cleaning Services
Deep Cleaning

Our Deep Clean is a thorough, top-to-bottom service designed for spaces that need a reset. We go beyond the surface—every corner, every fixture, every detail. Ideal as a first-time clean, seasonal refresh, or before/after a major event.

All Rooms
  • Wipe down all cabinets and drawers
  • Wipe down all blinds
  • Wipe down doors and walls (where dirty)
  • Hand wipe all baseboards
  • Vacuum all sofas and upholstered furniture
  • Dust and clean all ceiling fans
  • Dust all light fixtures
  • Clean all windowsills
Kitchen & Dining
  • Clean and sanitize countertops
  • Wipe down cabinets and drawers
  • Clean sink and faucet
  • Stove: clean top, front, and inside
  • Oven: clean inside and outside
  • Microwave: clean inside and outside
  • Refrigerator: clean outside and handles
  • Dishwasher: wipe down exterior
  • Vacuum and mop floors
Bathrooms
  • Scrub and sanitize toilet (bowl, seat, base)
  • Scrub shower/tub (tiles, grout, showerhead)
  • Clean sink and faucet
  • Wipe down mirrors
  • Clean counters and surfaces
  • Clean light fixtures
  • Vacuum and mop floors
Bedrooms & Living Areas
  • Dust and polish all surfaces
  • Vacuum and mop floors
  • Dust blinds or curtains
  • Make beds (fresh linens if provided)
  • Fluff and arrange cushions
  • Vacuum sofas and chairs

Supplies included. Andrea provides all professional-grade cleaning products and equipment. You don’t need to provide a thing.

Post-Construction Clean

Construction and renovation leave behind fine dust, debris, adhesive residue, and grime that standard cleaning can’t handle. Our post-construction clean makes the space truly move-in ready—not just presentable.

All Areas
  • Remove all construction dust from surfaces
  • Wipe down all cabinets, drawers, and shelving
  • Clean all windows (interior) and window tracks
  • Wipe down all doors, frames, and hardware
  • Hand wipe all baseboards and trim
  • Remove paint overspray and adhesive residue
  • Vacuum all vents and registers
  • Sweep, vacuum, and mop all floors
Kitchen
  • Clean inside all cabinets and drawers
  • Clean all appliances inside and out
  • Scrub sink and faucet
  • Clean countertops and backsplash
Bathrooms
  • Scrub tile, grout, and fixtures
  • Remove grout haze from tile surfaces
  • Clean toilets, sinks, tubs, and showers
  • Polish mirrors and glass

Custom quotes only. Post-construction pricing is based on square footage and scope. Contact us for a free on-site or phone estimate before booking.
